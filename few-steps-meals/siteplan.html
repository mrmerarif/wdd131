Site Name

Name: Few Steps Meals

Reason for this name: The name “Few Steps Meals” reflects the core idea of the site: simple, approachable recipes that can be made in just a few clear steps.

Optional domain idea: fewstepsmeals.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Few Steps Meals is to provide a focused, easy-to-use recipe hub for busy individuals and families who want home-cooked meals without complicated instructions.

  • Step-by-step recipes with minimal steps.
  • Meal ideas organized by categories.
  • Tips and blog-style articles.
  • A references page citing all external sources.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: “Where can I find quick dinner ideas that don’t use many ingredients?”
  • Scenario 2: “Where can I find simple recipes that are easy to follow?”
  • Scenario 3: “Which meals are budget-friendly for families?”
