Few Steps Meals – Website Planning Document

WDD 131 | Dynamic Web Fundamentals

Site Name

Name: Few Steps Meals

Reason for this name: The name “Few Steps Meals” reflects the core idea of the site: simple, approachable recipes that can be made in just a few clear steps. It emphasizes ease, clarity, and practicality for busy people who still want homemade food.

Optional domain idea: fewstepsmeals.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Few Steps Meals is to provide a focused, easy-to-use recipe hub for busy individuals and families who want home-cooked meals without complicated instructions or overwhelming ingredient lists. The site will offer:

  • Step-by-step recipes broken into clear, minimal steps.
  • Meal ideas organized by categories (budget-friendly, quick dinners, kid-approved, etc.).
  • Tips and blog-style articles to help users plan meals and reduce stress around cooking.
  • A references page citing all external sources used.

Scenarios

These are example questions a visitor to Few Steps Meals might have:

  • Scenario 1: “I get home from work tired and only have 30 minutes. Where can I find quick dinner ideas that don’t use a lot of ingredients?”
  • Scenario 2: “I’m new to cooking and easily overwhelmed by long recipes. Is there a place where I can see simple, step-by-step meals that are easy to follow?”
  • Scenario 3: “I’m on a budget and need affordable meal ideas for my family. Which recipes are budget-friendly and use pantry staples?”

Color Scheme

The color scheme for Few Steps Meals is designed to feel warm, calm, and approachable, while still looking clean and modern.

Primary

#1A2A40

Used for headings, header background, and key accents.

Secondary

#B6B09F

Used for navigation backgrounds and subtle accents.

Card Background

#EAE4D5

Used for cards, content blocks, and recipe panels.

Page Background

#F2F2F2

Used as the main page background.

Typography

The typography for Few Steps Meals is chosen to balance personality and readability.

  • Headings: Playfair Display Used for main titles and section headings.
  • Body Text: Roboto Used for paragraphs, lists, and general content.
