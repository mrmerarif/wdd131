About Me

I'm originally from Mexico. I love learning new things; lately I've been teaching myself piano and guitar, cooking for my family, playing with my kids, reading, and traveling. Mexico

Mexico is a country full of color, history, and tradition. I grew up surrounded by music, food, and celebrations that bring people together. From the ancient cultures like the Aztecs and Maya to the modern blend of art, family, and community, Mexico has a spirit that stays with you.
